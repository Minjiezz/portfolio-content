Landscapes

These are the landscape photos I took during my travels. When I saw the beautiful scenery, I was so immersed in the beautiful atmosphere that I recorded these moments with photos.

The first half of these photos come from a trip I made to Miami Beach on Christmas 2021. I recorded the sunset by the sea. The second half of these photos are from my trip to Sichuan, China, in the summer of 2021. I hope I can go to more places and record different scenery in the future.
